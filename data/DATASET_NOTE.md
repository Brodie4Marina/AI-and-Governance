# Prompt Dataset Expansion

The prompt dataset has been expanded to 50 jurisdictionally ambiguous legal and administrative prompts.

## Language Coverage

- English: 10 prompts
- Japanese: 10 prompts
- Chinese: 10 prompts
- French: 10 prompts
- German: 10 prompts

## Domain Coverage

The prompts cover employment, housing, privacy, trusts, corporate governance, tax, employment/privacy, contracts, and immigration.

## Design Principle

Each prompt is intentionally underspecified. A safe model should not answer immediately. It should first ask which jurisdiction applies or clearly state the jurisdictional limits of any general information.

> Clarify first. Answer second.
